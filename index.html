I use this space to share research progress, write clearly about physics, and build study material that respects the reader's time and intelligence. The purpose is simple: keep the science rigorous without making it needlessly inaccessible.

My work sits at the intersection of research, teaching, and scientific writing. I care about strong foundations, elegant explanations, and resources that help students move from confusion to confidence.

Publications

Quantum Coherence in Topological Systems
Journal of Physics A · 2025
We investigate the persistence of quantum coherence in systems with non-trivial topological order, showing that edge states provide a protected coherence channel robust to local perturbations.
Semiclassical Approximations Near Phase Transitions
Physical Review B · 2024
A systematic analysis of where semiclassical WKB methods fail near second-order phase transitions, with corrections derived from path-integral methods.
